About Me

Hello! My name is Ravindu Vinusha, a passionate 22-year-old undergraduate student pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at the General Sir John Kothelawela Defence University, along with a degree in Physical Science from the University of Sri Jayawardenapura. Hailing from the picturesque district of Badulla in Sri Lanka, I embarked on my academic journey with a keen interest in mathematics, completing my Advanced Level examinations in the Maths stream in 2021 and Ordinary Level examinations in 2018.

Driven by my curiosity and love for technology, I've embarked on various learning adventures, including completing courses such as Full Stack Development at the University of Moratuwa, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at SLIIT, and Graphic Designing at NYSCO. Additionally, I've honed my skills in networking through Cisco certification courses.

In the realm of freelancing, I've explored diverse opportunities on platforms like Fiverr and Upwork. My repertoire includes graphic designing, mobile, laptop, and PC repairing, robotics, and Arduino programming, web and app development, UI/UX designing, ethical hacking, and software development. Beyond the digital domain, I find solace in strumming the guitar, strategizing over a game of chess, and embracing the camaraderie of football matches.

Committed to sharing knowledge and nurturing talents, I also engage in tutoring sessions to impart my expertise to aspiring minds. With a vision to specialize in electronics within the field of computer engineering, I am driven by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and innovation.

Join me as I embark on this exhilarating journey of exploration, learning, and growth in the fascinating world of technology!
